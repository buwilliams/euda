# Personas

## Key Ideas

- **Agents Have Concerns**: a persona is a background agent defined by an area of interest—a *concern*—rather than a task list. Each carries its own identity.
- **A Personal Team**: personas form a team of intelligences serving different facets of a person's wellbeing, all evolving with the person they serve.
- **Quiet By Default**: personas work in the background and coordinate through topics. They surface what matters; they do not add noise.
- **A Growing Catalog**: the long-term vision is a catalog of personas for mental health, fitness, education, and more—each a pre-built identity a person can adopt and grow.
- **Same Composition As Any Agent**: a persona is just an agent—Identity + Cognition + Memory + Behavior—tuned to a concern. No special machinery.

## Purpose

Personas exist so that the many facets of a person's flourishing can each have a dedicated intelligence. A single generic assistant flattens everything into one voice and one concern. Euda instead lets distinct agents specialize: one watches what flows in, one reaches out to expand horizons, one mines data to learn the person. Together they form a team rather than a tool.

Because every persona has its own identity and evolves through consolidation, a person's team grows with them. The curator learns what to filter; the explorer learns what kind of growth resonates; each becomes more *theirs* over time.

## The Concerns

The defining personas express Euda's purpose directly:

- **Soul** — pattern recognition and opportunity. Sees what a person cannot see about themselves and turns patterns into care. See [Soul](01-soul.md).
- **Curator** — filters the incoming firehose from the outside world into a curated feed, so attention is guarded and echo chambers are broken. See [Curator](02-curator.md).
- **Explorer** — goes out into the world to find what would expand a person's horizons, aligned with their values, so they keep growing. See [Explorer](03-explorer.md).
- **Consolidate** — mines a person's data to learn them, feeding the consolidation engine. See [Consolidation](../03-consolidation.md).

These embody the "safety and surprise" balance: the curator builds walls that ground a person in their values; the explorer builds bridges to the unfamiliar that keep them from stagnating.

## Expected Role

Personas should be specialized agents that a person can add, configure, and grow:

- each has its own identity and concern;
- each works in the background via triggers and coordinates through topics;
- each evolves through consolidation alongside the person and the rest of the team.

In `v1`, the core protected agents are **Soul** (pattern recognition), **Chat** (the conversational router that can create and manage other agents), and **User** (the person, modeled as an agent). The broader catalog—curator, explorer, and wellbeing personas—is the architected direction the system is built to support.

Personas should not fragment the person's experience. They specialize behind the scenes; the person still meets one coherent intelligence through Focus.

## Future Direction

The long-term vision is a catalog of pre-built personas for domains of human wellbeing—mental health, physical fitness, education, relationships, professional growth—each shipping as an identity a person can adopt and then evolve uniquely. A person assembles the team that fits their life, and that team grows with them.

The intent to preserve: specialized agents, each with a concern and an evolving identity, working quietly in the background and coordinating through topics, in service of one person's flourishing.
